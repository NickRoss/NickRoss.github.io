A four-part workshop series developed jointly by the University of Chicago's [Career Advancement Office](https://careeradvancement.uchicago.edu/careers-in/artificial-intelligence/) and Data Science Institute for advanced undergraduates. The series progresses from foundational concepts through practical application: students learn how LLMs work and make API calls, build data pipelines, refine outputs through context engineering, and build agentic systems with tool use for autonomous decision-making.

Course materials: [Winter 2026](https://github.com/NickRoss/2026-AI-Development), [Spring 2026](https://github.com/NickRoss/2026-spring-AI-Development).
