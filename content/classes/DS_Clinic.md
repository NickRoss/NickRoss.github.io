The [Data Science Clinic](https://clinic.ds.uchicago.edu/) is an experiential, project-based course where students work in teams with real-world clients under the supervision of instructors. Students tackle authentic problems and deliver tangible outputs including data analysis, open source software, and final presentations and reports for their clients.
