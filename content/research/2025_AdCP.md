This article examines the practical economics of implementing agentic AI in advertising today, rather than focusing on speculative future capabilities. It introduces the Ad Context Protocol (AdCP) and argues that understanding where AI agents make financial sense today is the difference between a sustainable competitive advantage and burning through your margins.
