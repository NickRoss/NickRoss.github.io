While at TinyCo, we shared exclusive monetization data with Adweek showing that Google's Nexus 7 tablet significantly outperformed other Android devices. Key findings included 50% higher ARPU compared to standard Android tablets, 50% higher likelihood of in-app purchases, and approximately 20% higher 7- and 30-day retention rates. Despite representing only 15% of TinyCo's Android tablet users, Nexus 7 accounted for 30% of Android tablet revenue.
